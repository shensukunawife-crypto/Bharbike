Our Ride Workflow

Get riding in four easy steps using our intuitive mobile app.

1

Locate

Find available electric vehicles on the live interactive map in the app, parked at any nearby secure Hub station.

2

Book & Unlock

Recharge your wallet instantly, pick a flexible pass or subscription, and unlock the bike keylessly via Bluetooth or cellular.

3

Ride

Commute with a smart, eco-friendly EV. The app keeps track of your ride duration, distance, and battery health via real-time GPS.

4

Return & Lock

End your ride by parking at any designated BhaरBike hub and locking the smart lock directly from your mobile app screen.
