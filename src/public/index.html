Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? Find quick answers about unlocking, KYC, fares, and deposits.

How do I unlock a BhaरBike?

▼

Once you locate a bike near you on the BhaरBike mobile app, ensure your phone's Bluetooth is turned on. Pick a dynamic rental pass or weekly commute subscription, and slide to unlock the bike keylessly. The smart lock opens automatically in under 3 seconds.

What are the hourly, daily, and weekly rates?

▼

We offer flexible and transparent rates. Pay-Per-Ride starts at ₹49/hour. For longer rides, get our 4-hour block pass at ₹149, full 24-hour day pass at ₹349, or a 7-day retention pass at ₹1,499. Regular commuters can opt for the Weekly Commuter plan at ₹1,950 for 7 days.

What documents are required for KYC verification?

▼

To ensure safety and comply with local regulations, first-time users must verify their identity. You will need to upload a photo of your Aadhaar card, PAN, or a valid Driving License directly within the BhaरBike mobile app. Verification is usually completed in less than 5 minutes.

Is the security deposit refundable?

▼

Yes! First-time users pay a registration deposit of ₹1,500, which includes a ₹1,000 one-time registration fee and ₹500 that is fully refundable back to your bank account or payment method immediately upon returning the bike to any designated hub and completing your subscription.
